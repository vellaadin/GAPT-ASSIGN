What is this?

placeholdername is an emotion-driven music player that uses your webcam to detect and identify your facial expressions using a machine learning model and plays music based on your mood.

How does it work?

placeholdername makes use of your webcam feed to find your face using OpenCV and then uses a machine learning model trained using Google Teachable Machine to identify your facial expression. The model then uses this information to play music from a curated selection based on your mood.

Why was it made?

placeholdername was made as a project for the GAPT course at the University of Malta. It was made to demonstrate the use of machine learning and computer vision in a real world application.
